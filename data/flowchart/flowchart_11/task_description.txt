**Objective:**  
Design a logical process to manage the employee expense reimbursement workflow.  

**Problem Specification:**  
- **Process Input:** The process must accept employee expense claims, including receipts, descriptions of expenses, and the total amount claimed.  
- **Process Output:** The process must yield a final decision on each expense claim, indicating whether it is approved, rejected, or requires additional information.  
- **Constraints / Rules:**  
  1. All expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.  
  2. Claims must include valid receipts for all expenses over a specified amount.  
  3. Claims must be reviewed by a designated manager or finance officer.  
  4. If a claim is rejected, the employee must be notified with reasons for the rejection.  
  5. The process must allow for appeals or resubmissions of rejected claims.
