## Problem Statement
### Objective  
Design a flowchart to represent the triage process in an emergency room, focusing on categorizing and prioritizing patients based on the urgency of their medical conditions.

### Background or Scenario  
In a hospital's emergency room, patients arrive with various medical conditions that require assessment to determine the urgency of their treatment. The triage process is crucial for ensuring that patients receive appropriate and timely care based on the severity of their conditions.

### Problem Specification  
#### Input or Requirements  
- Patients with varying medical conditions.
- A triage nurse to assess the condition of each patient.
- Defined triage categories: Critical, Urgent, Non-Urgent.
- Available medical staff and resources in the emergency room.

#### Constraints or Rules  
- Patients must be categorized quickly to ensure timely treatment.
- Limited staff and resources necessitate efficient prioritization.
- Triage decisions must be based on medical urgency and available resources.
