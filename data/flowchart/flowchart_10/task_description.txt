**Objective:**  
Design a logical process to outline the legal steps involved in tenant eviction due to non-payment of rent.  

**Problem Specification:**  
- **Process Input:**  
The process must accept information regarding the tenant's rental agreement, the amount of rent due, and any prior communication regarding payment issues.  
- **Process Output:**  
The process must yield a clear sequence of steps that a landlord must follow to legally evict a tenant for non-payment, including any necessary documentation and legal notices.  
- **Constraints / Rules:**  
1. The process must comply with local landlord-tenant laws and regulations.  
2. The landlord must provide written notice to the tenant before initiating eviction proceedings.  
3. The process must include steps for potential tenant responses or defenses against eviction.
