### Objective:
Design a logical process to outline a project workflow from the planning stage to completion.

### Problem Specification:

**Process Input:**
The process must accept any project proposal that includes objectives, timelines, resources, and stakeholder requirements.

**Process Output:**
The process must yield a comprehensive workflow that guides the project from initial planning through to successful completion, including all necessary stages and decision points.

**Constraints / Rules:**
1. The process must include stages for initial planning, resource allocation, execution, monitoring, and completion.
2. Each stage must have defined decision points that determine the progression to the next stage or the need for adjustments.
3. The process must account for potential risks and include contingency planning.
4. The process must ensure stakeholder communication and feedback mechanisms are in place throughout the project lifecycle.
5. The process must be adaptable to different project sizes and complexities.
