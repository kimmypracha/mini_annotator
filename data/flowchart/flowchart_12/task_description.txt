## Problem Statement
### Objective  
Design a flowchart to guide the process of enrolling in courses at a university.

### Background or Scenario  
At a university, students need to enroll in courses each semester. The process involves checking course prerequisites, ensuring availability, and adhering to enrollment timelines.

### Problem Specification  
#### Input or Requirements  
- Students' academic records, including completed courses and current academic standing.
- List of available courses for the semester, including prerequisites, credits, and capacity.
- Academic calendar specifying enrollment open and close dates.
- Enrollment system interface for managing course selection and registration.

#### Constraints or Rules  
- Enrollment must occur within the specified dates in the academic calendar.
- Students must satisfy all prerequisites for a course before enrolling.
- Course capacity limits must not be exceeded.
- Students are restricted to enrolling in a maximum number of credits per semester.
