## Problem Statement
### Objective  
Design a flowchart to guide the process of cooking Korean ground beef using only the provided ingredients.

### Background or Scenario  
You want to prepare a Korean-inspired ground beef dish for dinner using only the ingredients you currently have in your kitchen. The goal is to create a flavorful and satisfying meal that can be served with rice.

### Problem Specification
#### Input or Requirements  
- Ground beef: 1 pound  
- Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons  
- Brown sugar: 2 tablespoons  
- Garlic: 2 cloves  
- Ginger: 1 teaspoon (grated)  
- Green onions: 2 stalks  
- Sesame oil: 1 tablespoon  
- Rice: 2 cups (cooked)  
- Optional: Red pepper flakes to taste  

#### Constraints or Rules  
- Use only the ingredients provided.  
- The cooking process should be completed within 45 minutes.  
- The dish should be suitable for at least 2 servings.  
- Ensure the final dish has a balance of flavors typical of Korean cuisine.
