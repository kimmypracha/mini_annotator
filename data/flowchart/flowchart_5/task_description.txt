### Objective:
Design a logical process to outline the hiring journey from a candidate's perspective, starting from the submission of a job application.

### Problem Specification:

**Process Input:**
- The process must begin with the submission of a job application by a candidate.

**Process Output:**
- The process must yield a clear sequence of steps and potential decisions the candidate might face, leading to a final hiring decision or rejection.

**Constraints / Rules:**
- The process should include common stages such as application review, interviews (phone, video, or in-person), assessments or tests, and final decision-making.
- The process must account for possible feedback or requests for additional information from the candidate.
- The process should consider scenarios where the candidate might need to follow up or withdraw their application.
- The process should be adaptable to different company policies and practices, recognizing that not all steps may apply to every situation.
