## Problem  

### Domain Context  
French Language Learning encompasses the study of vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, and listening comprehension necessary for understanding and communicating in French. It is intended for beginners or intermediate learners who aim to build a foundational understanding of the language. This domain includes the basic linguistic elements and tools that facilitate language acquisition but excludes advanced linguistic analysis or cultural immersion techniques.

### Guiding Questions  
1. What are the main components of French vocabulary that beginners should focus on?  
2. How do fundamental French grammar rules differ from those in English?  
3. What are the key elements of effective pronunciation in French?  
4. How can listening skills be developed to improve comprehension in French?
