## Problem  

### Domain Context  
Social Media Marketing is a domain that encompasses the strategic use of social media platforms to promote products, services, or brands. It involves content planning, platform-specific tactics, audience engagement, and performance tracking. This field is intended for marketing professionals or advanced students who are interested in the strategic aspects of using social media for marketing purposes. Topics outside this domain include traditional advertising methods and non-digital marketing strategies.  

### Guiding Questions  
1. What are the main components of a successful social media marketing strategy?  
2. How does content planning differ across various social media platforms?  
3. What tactics are most effective for engaging with audiences on different social media platforms?  
4. What metrics are essential for tracking the performance of social media marketing efforts?
