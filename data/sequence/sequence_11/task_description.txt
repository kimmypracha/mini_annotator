## Objective
Create a sequence diagram that illustrates the interactions between a passenger, a driver, and the system within a ride-sharing service. The diagram should detail the process from the initial ride request by the passenger to the completion of payment after the journey.

## Expected Diagram Format
Sequence Diagram: A sequence diagram is a type of interaction diagram that shows how processes operate with one another and in what order. It depicts the sequence of messages exchanged between objects or components to carry out a function or a process.

## Constraints
- The diagram must include the following interactions:
  - Ride request initiated by the passenger.
  - Driver matching process conducted by the system.
  - Journey tracking during the ride.
  - Completion of payment after the ride ends.
