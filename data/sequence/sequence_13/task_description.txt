## Objective
Create a sequence diagram that illustrates the automated inventory restocking process in a retail store. The diagram should depict the interactions between different components involved in monitoring inventory levels, placing orders with suppliers, and confirming the delivery of stock.

## Expected Diagram Format
Sequence Diagram: A type of diagram that shows how objects interact in a particular scenario of a business process by depicting the sequence of messages exchanged between them over time.

## Constraints
- The diagram must include steps for inventory monitoring.
- It should show the process of ordering from suppliers.
- Delivery confirmation steps must be represented.
