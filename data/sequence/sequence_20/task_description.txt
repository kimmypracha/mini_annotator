## Objective
Create a sequence diagram that illustrates the process of initiating and joining a video conference call. The diagram should detail the interactions between different components and participants involved in the process, from the creation of the meeting to the setup of media streaming.

## Expected Diagram Format
Sequence Diagram: A type of diagram that shows how objects interact in a particular scenario of a use case. It focuses on the order of messages exchanged between the objects to achieve a specific outcome.

## Constraints
- The diagram must include the following steps:
  - Meeting creation process.
  - Invitation of participants to the meeting.
  - Establishment of connections between participants and the conference system.
  - Setup of media streaming for the video call.
